Invest in Buzzineses

In today’s busy day and age, the everyday investor does not have time to conduct the 20+ hours of necessary due diligence to understand a company’s financial health and the intricacies of their business model. Consequently, the stock market has become more reactive to news, or “buzz” surrounding a company rather behind-the-scene changes to supply chain or restructuring that affects profit. Take Tesla, whose volatile stock prices can be in part attributed to the eccentricness of Elon Musk. BuzzLine gathers historical data and uses machine learning to determine the past correlation between “buzz” and the stock price of a specific company. Then BuzzLine will out a “Buzz Score” that is a recommendation for whether you should buy, sell, or hold.
